Project Eiden

As a scientist, you probably generate more research ideas than you can pursue. Here you will be able to get credit for your original ideas and projects and borrow ideas of others. Our goal is to make the exchange of ideas as smooth as possible and to facilitate fast and accurate feedback on research projects. Here you can read a more detailed project's description. Are you interested? Then please give us your email address and you will be among the first to know when we launch.
